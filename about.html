About Smart College Management Portal

Smart College Management Portal is a web-based application developed using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. It helps students and teachers manage academic information in an easy and organized way. This portal includes Student Registration, Login, Dashboard, Attendance Management, Marks Calculator, Timetable, Library, Placement Cell, Contact Form and many other useful features. The main objective of this project is to make college management simple, fast and user-friendly.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide a modern digital platform for students and teachers.

👨‍🎓 Students

Students can check attendance, marks, timetable and notices online.

💻 Technology

Developed using HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript with Responsive Design.
